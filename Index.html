Our Story

Born on the fairway.
Built by golfers.

If you've ever navigated junior tournament golf, you know the drill. You're juggling five different tour websites, a dozen spreadsheets, email newsletters, Facebook groups, and a calendar that somehow still doesn't have all the dates right.

You spend hours every week trying to figure out which tournaments are the right fit for your player's age, skill level, and goals — only to guess wrong or miss registration deadlines entirely.

We built TourMap because we lived that frustration ourselves. As golfers, we knew there had to be a better way to plan a season. So we built it.

Today, we aggregate tournaments from HJGT, TYGA, SCJGT, DGT, AJGA, PGA Jr., CGA, Pinehurst Junior, US Kids, and USGA into one clean, searchable platform — and we layer on the insights you actually need to compete smarter.

Common Questions

Good questions,
straight answers.

Why is it annual-only? Can I pay monthly?
Junior golf is a year-round commitment, and so is keeping the platform current. Annual pricing lets us stay focused on building the best tool for families — and it means you're fully covered from the first day of the season to the last.
What exactly do I get for $500?
Full access to everything — tournaments across 20+ junior golf tour organizations, the season schedule builder, results history, deadline reminders, rankings tracking, and personalized recommendations. There's no higher tier because there is no higher tier.
Does one account cover multiple kids?
Each account is for one child. If you have multiple junior golfers, you'll need a separate membership for each player. This keeps schedules, results, and recommendations accurate and personal for each child.
When does my membership renew?
Your membership renews one year from your sign-up date. We'll email you a heads-up before it renews. You can cancel at any time and keep full access through the end of your paid year — no prorating, no hassle.
What tournaments do you cover?
We track events from HJGT, TYGA, SCJGT, DGT, AJGA, PGA Jr., CGA, Pinehurst Junior, US Kids Golf, and USGA — all in one searchable, filterable dashboard covering North and South Carolina.
Do tournament entry fees go through you?
No. We're a planning and scheduling tool — entry fees are paid directly to each tour organization on their own site. We surface all the details and deadlines so you know exactly where to go and when.
What if I'm not satisfied?
Reach out within the first 30 days and we'll make it right. We're a small team and we'd rather have a conversation than an unhappy member.
